About MCSF

We are a vibrant Christian community dedicated to nurturing students spiritually, academically, and socially.

Our Vision

To raise a generation of students who are firmly rooted in Christ, excelling in their academics, and making positive impacts in their communities.

Our Mission

To provide spiritual guidance, academic support, and character development programs that equip students to fulfill their God-given potential.
