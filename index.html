About MCSF

We are a vibrant Christian community dedicated to nurturing students spiritually, academically, and socially.

MCSF Vision Statement

MCSF is committed to seeing an uprisings of firebrand Youths, Ambassadors and Ministers of God whose impact will be felt beyond the campus. At MCSF, we target the making/raising of Kingdom artilleries who will be generational change makers for God’s Kingdom.

Our Mission

•To bring men unto Salvation through the propagation of God's word throughout our Campus and beyond (Luke 10:19, John 3:17) (SAVED BY GOD) •To stir up genuine Change and Revival in the lives of men through core discipleship, and the baptism of the Holy Spirit (Gen 1:26, Matthew 4:19, 2 Corinthians 3:18) (MADE LIKE GOD) •To raise passionate soul winners who will unashamedly witness for God and see that all won souls followed up until firmly established in Christ!(John 20:21, Acts1:8, Acts 10:38) (SENT BY GOD)
